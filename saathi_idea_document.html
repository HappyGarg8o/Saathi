The problem
Millions of young Indians in metro cities are surrounded by people — and still deeply, quietly lonely. They have no one to grab lunch with. No one to catch a movie with. No one to just sit and talk to without it becoming complicated.
Relocated professionals
Moved to Bangalore or Mumbai for work. No social circle yet. Weekends feel empty.
Social anxiety
Want to go out but dread going alone. Need a low-pressure companion to make it easier.
Post-breakup / divorce
Re-entering social life after a relationship ends. Need company without romance pressure.
Event plus-one
Wedding, office party, family function — needs a companion but doesn't want to ask awkwardly.
Solo travellers
Visiting a new city. Wants a local to explore with, not a tour guide — a real companion.
Busy friend circles
Everyone's schedule is packed. Wants to do something but all friends are unavailable.
Why existing options fail
Dating apps
Tinder, Bumble — built for romance. Using them just for company feels dishonest and creates wrong expectations. People ghost. It gets complicated.
Wrong intentRomantic pressure
Friend apps

No commitmentToo slow
RentAFriend.com
The only real competitor — but it's built in 2009. No mobile app, no safety features, no India focus, no UPI, no local trust. Completely outdated.

Therapy / wellness apps

The solution — Saathi
What Saathi does
Saathi is a two-sided marketplace. On one side are companions — verified, rated, real people who earn money by spending quality time with others. On the other side are users who book them for specific social activities with a clear time, place, and purpose. No ambiguity. No romantic expectation. Just genuine company.

What makes Saathi different
Safety-first design
Aadhaar-linked ID verification, live location sharing, SOS emergency button, pre-booking video intro, and 24/7 support. Built for India's reality.
Zero ambiguity
Every booking has a defined activity, location, duration, and cost. Both parties know exactly what they signed up for before they meet.
India-native payments
UPI, Razorpay, instant companion payouts. No international card friction. Designed ground-up for how India actually pays.
Quality companions
Companions are verified, trained on platform etiquette, and rated after every session. Bad actors are removed automatically.
